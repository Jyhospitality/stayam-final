Overview

Welcome To Hotel Satyam Inn Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh. Hotel Satyam Inn is clearly the best-located luxury hotel in the divine land of Lord Mahakal. Strategically situated in the heart of the bustling and colourful city of Ujjain, the bus stand, taxi stand, and shopping arcades are all accessible easily. It is one of the budget-friendly hotels near Mahakaleshwar temple.

At the Hotel Satyam, we offer you flexibility and comfort with 3-star services and facilities as well as the traditional warmth of Madhya Pradesh. So whether you are in Ujjain for a Spiritual or Holiday Purpose, make the city palace your home with Satyam Inn Hotel
